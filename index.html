About Our Cafe

Baan Cafe Pee Dear brings you the authentic taste of Thai coffee culture. Our name, which means "Dear Uncle's House Cafe" in Thai, reflects our warm, family-oriented atmosphere where every guest is treated like family.

We serve traditional Thai coffee alongside modern specialty drinks, all prepared with locally sourced beans and time-honored brewing methods. Our cozy space combines traditional Thai design elements with contemporary comfort, creating the perfect environment for relaxation and connection.

Whether you're looking for a strong Thai iced coffee to start your day or a peaceful corner to enjoy our signature drinks, Baan Cafe Pee Dear is your home away from home.
